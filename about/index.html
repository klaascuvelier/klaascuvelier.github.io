About me

I am Klaas Cuvelier.

After graduating in 2009 with a degree in Applied Informatics and completing an internship at Netlog, I joined Netlog as front-end web-developer. In 2012, I became one of the founding engineers at Showpad, where I currently serve as a principal software engineer in the Developer Platform Team.

In addition to my passion for software development and technology, I also enjoy spending time outdoors, particularly through running and hiking. I have documented some of my experiences on my running page.
